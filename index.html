About the host

Meet the man in the room.

Dr. Stephen Akintayo is the Chairman of SACI Holdings, a global private equity firm with interests in real estate, technology and emerging markets across Africa, the UAE, the UK and the USA. He is the Founder and President of the Stephen Akintayo Foundation, and the founder of Gtext Holdings, a multinational conglomerate he built from the ground up. In 2023, Forbes named him the Best of Africa's Leading Investment Coach & Real Estate Mogul. He's a member of the Forbes Business Council, the author of 45+ books on wealth and entrepreneurship, and host of The Billionaire Habits Podcast. But the part that matters most: he started with nothing. From a thrift collector on campus to a recharge-card stand in Abuja, his story is proof that the gap between where you are and where you want to be is closed by the right knowledge — and the right rooms. His mission is bold and specific: to raise one million millionaires by 2050. On 24th July, a few of them could be sitting in Glasgow.
